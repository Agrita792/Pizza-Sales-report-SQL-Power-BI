## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing pizza sales data using **SQL Server Management Studio (SSMS)** and **Power BI** to uncover actionable business insights related to revenue, sales performance, customer ordering behavior, product popularity, and operational trends.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive business intelligence dashboard that enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions regarding inventory planning, product strategy, and sales optimization.

## Business Insights

### Revenue Analysis

* Generated over **$722K+ revenue** during the analysis period.
* Large-sized pizzas contributed approximately **46% of total revenue**, making them the most profitable size category.
* Classic pizzas generated the highest revenue contribution among all pizza categories.

### Customer Ordering Trends

* Fridays recorded the highest number of orders, indicating increased weekend demand.
* January and July experienced peak order volumes compared to other months.

### Product Performance

* The Thai Chicken Pizza emerged as the highest revenue-generating product.
* The Classic Deluxe Pizza recorded the highest quantity sold and total orders.
* The Brie Carre Pizza consistently ranked among the lowest-performing products across revenue, quantity, and orders.

### Operational Recommendations

* Increase inventory allocation for top-performing pizzas.
* Launch promotional campaigns for underperforming products.
* Optimize staffing and inventory during high-demand days such as Fridays and weekends.
* Focus marketing efforts around peak-performing categories and pizza sizes.
